"Formed" and "Franciscan at Home"

Our parish offers subscriptions to "Formed" and "Franciscan at Home". Parishioners are encouraged to create their own accounts and explore these valuable platforms for spiritual growth and reflection.

For "Formed":

  • Visit formed.org
  • Click "Find Your Parish Subscription"
  • Search for "Canadian Martyrs Catholic Church" to register and create your account

For "Franciscan at Home":

  • Go to franciscanathome.com
  • Select "Partnering Dioceses"
  • Choose "Archdiocese of Vancouver, British Columbia" and register there

Please take advantage of these free resources to deepen your spiritual journey and enrich your faith life.

Archbishop Miller's Pastoral Letter on the Jubilee of Hope

Dear Brothers and Sisters in Christ, The Jubilee Year of Hope is a profoundly significant and grace-filled year for our Archdiocese. It offers us unique opportunities to deepen our relationship with God and one another. Pope Francis encourages us to be “pilgrims of hope” on our journey to the Father's House (cf. John 14:2). In this Pastoral Letter, I wish to share with you the blessings of this Jubilee Year and inspire you to strengthen your hope in Christ. Read the full letter.
