Welcome to Canadian Martyrs Catholic Church!

We warmly invite you to our Church, a vibrant and welcoming community of faith in Richmond, BC. As part of the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Vancouver, we are dedicated to living out the Gospel of Jesus Christ through worship, education, and service.


Our Church is a place where you can deepen your relationship with God, find support in a caring community, and engage in meaningful acts of charity and compassion. Whether you are a lifelong Catholic or exploring faith for the first time, you will find a home here.


Join us for Mass, participate in our many ministries and programs, and be part of our journey of faith.


We look forward to welcoming you and growing together in Christ's love.



我们诚挚邀请您来到我们的堂区——位于卑诗省列治文的一个充满活力与热情的信仰团体。作为温哥华总教区的一部分，我们致力于通过礼仪敬拜、信仰培育与爱德服务，实践耶稣基督的福音精神。


我们的堂区是一个让您加深与天主关系的地方，在这里，您可以在关怀备至的团体中获得支持，并参与富有意义的爱德与关怀行动。无论您是终身信友，还是初次探索信仰的人，您都能在这里找到属于自己的家。


欢迎您参与弥撒，加入我们多元的善会与各项活动，与我们一同走在信仰的道路上。


我们期待您的到来，并在基督的爱内一同成长。
